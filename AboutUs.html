About CookingIna

CookingIna is your go-to online destination for discovering the rich, diverse, and vibrant flavors of Filipino cuisine. Whether you're a seasoned home cook or just beginning your culinary journey, we aim to bring the heart of the Philippines straight into your kitchen with easy-to-follow recipes, expert tips, and cooking inspiration.

Our Story

At CookingIna, we believe that food is more than just nourishment—it's a celebration of culture, tradition, and family. Founded by a group of Filipino food enthusiasts, we created this platform to share the authentic tastes of our heritage with the world. From the bustling streets of Manila to the serene shores of Cebu, Filipino food is as diverse as the country itself. Our mission is to capture that essence and make it accessible to anyone, anywhere.

What We Offer

  • Authentic Filipino Recipes: Explore a wide range of classic and contemporary Filipino dishes, from the comforting Sinigang na Baboy to the festive Lechon Kawali. We cover everything from simple everyday meals to elaborate holiday feasts.
  • Step-by-Step Guides: Our recipes are designed with home cooks in mind. Each one is broken down into easy-to-follow instructions, complete with tips on techniques, ingredient substitutions, and the best cooking tools to use.
  • Ingredient Spotlights: We highlight key Filipino ingredients, their uses, and how to find them locally or online, making it easier for you to recreate dishes at home.

Our Values

  • Authenticity: We are committed to delivering genuine Filipino recipes that stay true to their roots while also embracing modern twists and innovations in the kitchen.
  • Accessibility: Cooking should be fun, not intimidating. We provide detailed recipes that are easy to follow, with ingredients that can be found in your local market or online.
  • Cultural Pride: Filipino cuisine is a reflection of the country's rich history, diverse culture, and warmth. We take pride in sharing these flavors with the world and celebrating what makes Filipino food so special.
